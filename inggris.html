Mindmap of Fengchia nightmarket

During my time in Fengchia nightmarket, I have answered a couple of questions made by me before going there these questions are:
What do they sell there? Bite size steak, Potato with cheese & Gas Station drink stand
Are the food delicious? Quite delicious in my opinion
Are the food there only available to that nightmarket? Only a few are like the Gas Station drink stand
Are the prices expensive? Not too expensive in total I spent around $250 ntd
How crowded is this place? Not too crowded, not so much until it's jamed-pack
How loud is this place? Not loud at all only, the music played by the stores there that makes the place noisy
What kind of decorations can be found there? There wasn't really much decorations to the part of the nightmarket I went to, only screens promoting
What souvenirs can I buy there? You can buy many things like electronics, keychains, toys, and more
How big is the nightmarket? It is said to be the biggest in Taiwan
Is the path complicated? Not really, the part of the nightmarket I walk around was like the perimeter of a pizza!
